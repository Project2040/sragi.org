## **‚úÖ 1\. SRAGI Standard: 6 Image Ratios**

We use **six aspect ratios** ‚Äì no more, no less ‚Äì to maintain visual coherence across all platforms.

### **A) 3:2 (Primary Standard)**

**Typical resolutions:**

* 2400√ó1600  
* 3000√ó2000  
* 3600√ó2400

**Use cases:**

* Universal format for article heroes  
* SRAGI-TV / presentations  
* A4-friendly printing  
* Large illustrations

**Why this is primary:** Best balance between vertical and horizontal space. Works everywhere.

---

### **B) 16:9 (Widescreen)**

**Typical resolutions:**

* 1920√ó1080  
* 2560√ó1440  
* 3840√ó2160 (4K)

**Use cases:**

* YouTube / SRAGI-TV  
* Video thumbnails  
* Hero sections requiring widescreen  
* Presentations in 16:9 format

---

### **C) 1:1 (Square)**

**Typical resolutions:**

* 1080√ó1080  
* 1500√ó1500  
* 2048√ó2048

**Use cases:**

* Instagram feed (optimal)  
* Profile pictures  
* SRAGI Visual Tokens  
* Symbols and icons  
* Short information tiles

---

### **D) 4:5 (Vertical Social)**

**Typical resolutions:**

* 1080√ó1350  
* 1600√ó2000

**Use cases:**

* Instagram feed  
* Facebook mobile  
* LinkedIn mobile  
* Posters / story-format without text

**Important:** This format gives 25‚Äì35% better visibility on IG/FB compared to 1:1.

---

### **E) 9:16 (Vertical Video / Stories)**

**Typical resolutions:**

* 1080√ó1920  
* 1440√ó2560

**Use cases:**

* Instagram Reels  
* TikTok  
* Stories (IG, FB, LinkedIn)  
* Mobile-first promo

---

### **F) 4:3 (Classic)**

**Typical resolutions:**

* 1600√ó1200  
* 2048√ó1536

**Use cases:**

* Legacy screens  
* PDF documents (some cases)  
* "Between formats" for more square feeling

**Note:** This is optional but useful for certain document formats.
