## Core Principles Guiding Our Journey

### 1. R > 1 Always

Every project, every partnership, every decision is tested against the regenerative ratio:

**Does this give more than it takes?**

If no ‚Üí we don't do it.

### 2. Open by Default

Knowledge is not property. It's a **commons**.

- All frameworks: Open source
- All documentation: Freely available
- All learning: Shared openly

We use **CC BY-SA 4.0** and **SRL** to ensure regenerative reciprocity.

### 3. Human-in-the-Loop

AI is powerful, but **humans must remain the conductors**.

We will never:
- Automate ethical judgment
- Replace human wisdom with algorithms
- Build AI that operates without human oversight

### 4. Integrity Over Growth

We will not sacrifice:
- Ethics for scale
- Quality for speed
- Wholeness for profit

**We grow at the pace of integrity.**

### 5. Collaboration Over Competition

We see other regenerative initiatives as **allies, not competitors**.

- We share what we learn
- We credit our sources
- We build bridges, not walls
