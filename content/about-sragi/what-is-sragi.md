# What is SRAGI?

It's not just another AI tool ‚Äî it's a regenerative operating system for consciousness, technology, and Earth.

## The Core Principle: R > 1

At the heart of SRAGI lies a simple, profound equation:

**R > 1**

Where R is the **Regenerative Ratio** ‚Äî the relationship between what a system gives and what it takes.

- **R < 1** = Extractive (takes more than it gives)
- **R = 1** = Sustainable (balanced, but static)
- **R > 1** = Regenerative (gives more than it takes)

SRAGI systems are designed to strengthen the fabric of life itself.
